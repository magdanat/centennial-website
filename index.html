THE CENTENNIAL

The Filipino American Student Association at the University of Washington (FASA sa UW) as we know it today, is only one piece of the network of Filipino organizations on campus. It is a part of an evolution that started in October of 1917 through the Pensionados.

From one, we became many. Throughout the 2017-2018 school year, the Filipino community at the University of Washington (Seattle campus) will be celebrating the collective experience of Filipinos on campus. The Centennial Celebration serves as a platform to honor our past, spread awareness and appreciation about the Filipino culture and Filipino American history on and off campus, and most importantly, to rekindle, strengthen, and establish new bonds to create an intergenerational and diverse network.
