THE CENTENNIAL KICK-OFF

Event: The Centennial Kick-Off
Date: Saturday, September 23rd, 2017
Time: 2:00PM - 5:00PM
Location: UW "wǝɫǝbʔaltxʷ" Intellectual House
Address: 4249 Whitman Court, University of Washington, Seattle Campus, Seattle, Washington 98195


100 Years of established Filipino and Filipino-American organizations at the University of Washington (UW) Seattle! The Filipino American Student Association of UW (FASA sa UW) is proud to sponsor & present: The Centennial Celebration – 100 Years of Rising Up. Through the 2017- 2018 school year, we will be celebrating the collective experience of Filipinos on campus and in the community since its establishment in 1917. We openly invite all members of the community, past/current Filipino members, community allies, friends and family in our first all-inclusive Centennial Kick-off event! Join us in our fun-filled day with food, activities, networking, raffling, and performances! Since the first Filipino organization was est. in 1917, our community has been expanding & evolving. Through the years, we have been making an impact on campus and our surrounding communities. Learn more about our Centennial milestone, upcoming events, projects, and how to get involved in celebrating 100 years of Filipinos on campus.

THE CENTENNIAL GALA

Event: The Centennial Gala
Date: the month of May (TBA)
Time: TBA
Location: TBA
Address: TBA


Join us in a spectacular night as we top the year with our flagship event: The Centennial Gala! The last event of our centennial celebration, we will be celebrating our 100 years of Filipino legacy and the continuance of rising up in our legacy through a wonderful formal dinner. A night filled with cultural performance, food, auction, raffles, and warm company of supporting members and Filipino alumnus alike. All efforts made through the event will go towards supporting a scholarship fund to support the higher education of Filipino sa UW students. This will be a night you won’t forget.
