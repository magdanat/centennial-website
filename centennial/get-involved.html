GET INVOLVED

VOLUNTEER

Our talented volunteers are the forefront of our Centennial Celebration program! The Centennial Celebration program is completely ran by our amazing volunteers. We openly welcome new volunteers throughout the year to become a part of the team!


By volunteering for the Centennial Celebration year, you will have the opportunity to partake behind the scenes in our various events & projects. This is a fantastic chance to meet new friends, learn more about the Filipino community, and build on various valuable skills.


If reading this or attending one of our events has sparked a fire in you to get involved, please reach out to us at fasasauw.centennial@gmail.com.

BE A PARTNER

The Filipino community at the University of Washington (UW) Seattle is currently home to 15 Filipino organizations as of 2017. Many of our organizations work closely together with other groups and clubs on campus and around the Seattle area. The collective experience of Filipinos on campus for the past 100 years would have not be same if it weren’t for our past and current partners.


As FASA sa UW help celebrate 100 years of FIlipino presence at UW, we are on the lookout for new partners. We are constantly growing our network and resources as we discover new members and organizations to partner with. We recognize our partnership as a mutual relationship where our members work together and support each other towards a common goal. It is our values that tie us together. As partners, we can grow and become stronger together as one.

BE A SPONSOR

By becoming a sponsor, you are supporting the success of the Filipino community at the University of Washington Seattle. The Centennial Celebration is a large and vital program to the 2017-2018 academic school year. With FASA sa UW as its main sponsor, we are seeking to build upon our sponsorship to support the continuation of our celebration. As a sponsor, you will be recognized throughout our social media channels, websites, and events & projects. All sponsorship money will go towards the Centennial Celebration to support the production of our program. It is our mission to honor the past 100 years of Filipino legacy made on the University of Washington campus.
