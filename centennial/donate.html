DONATIONS

DONATE TO THE CENTENNIAL CELEBRATION

The Centennial Celebration is 110% ran and supported by our community members, partners, sponsors, and volunteers. Our program would not be made possible if it weren’t for the generous contributions of our community members!


You too, can help make an impact in our Filipino community at the University of Washington by donating through https://www.washington.edu/giving/make-a-gift/ . Go into the "Search" bar and type in "fasa" to create a donation to us! All donations will go towards our Centennial Celebration to help support the development of our events & projects throughout the 2017-2018 school year.


For further questions or inquiries please contact us at fasasauw.centennial@gmail.com .
