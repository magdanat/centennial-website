100 STORIES

Within 100 years of Filipino and Filipino American experience at the University of Washington, there are more than 100 stories to be told. We decided to share hand-picked stories showcasing the different impacts that Filipinos and Filipino American organizations had on each individual.

If you would like to share your own stories and/or pictures, please email them to anchee@uw.edu with “100 stories” as the subject line.
